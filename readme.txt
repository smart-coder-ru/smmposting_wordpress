== Description ==
**Social Media Auto-Posting and Scheduling Plugin for WordPress Sites and Blogs.**
Autopost, cross-promote, schedule and automatically share your posts to social networks

= Supported Networks = for cross-posting and auto posting

* **Odnoklassniki** - post to your page or in groups
* **Vkontakte** - post to your page or in groups
* **Telegram** - post to your page or in groups
* **Instagram** - Post to your Instagram profile
* **Facebook** - Post to your groups
* **Twitter** - Post to your Twitter account
* **Tumblr** - post to your blog post to your original post

== Installation ==
1. Go to the Admin Panel of your WordPress dashboard
2. Select “Plugin” -> “Add New” in the left hand side menu
3. Enter "SMMposting" in the "Search Plugins" text field
4. Click "Install Now" on the SMMposting banner
5. If needed enter your FTP data to install the plugin
6. After the installation click "Activate Plugin"
7. "SMMposting" appears in the left hand side menu
